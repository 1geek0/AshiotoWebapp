About

Ashioto was conceptualized during the 'KumbhaThon' workshop organized by MIT Media Lab in Jan 2014.
It was held in Nashik, India to solve problems at Kumbh Mela - the world's largest crowd gathering.
It is an intelligent crowd counting system which draws insights from foot traffic data.
