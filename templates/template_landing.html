About

In the midst of all the chaos and disorder of Kumbh Mela, 2014, Ashioto was concieved. With about one crore devotees expected to descend on Nashik on each of the parvani days- to take a holy dip - during the Kumbh Mela, crowd management was the most challenging part of organising this mega event. Few young innovators from Nashik decided to seize this opportunity and give a solution to this unsolved challenge by leveraging technology and design. Ashioto is an intelligent crowd counting system which draws insights from foot traffic data.
